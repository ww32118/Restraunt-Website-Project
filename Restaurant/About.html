About Us - Urban Spice Delight

Welcome to Urban Spice Delight, a culinary haven where exquisite flavors and vibrant cultures unite to create an unforgettable dining experience. Founded on January 15th, 2005 by culinary enthusiasts Akash Tyagi and Derek Williamson, our restaurant has become a cherished gem nestled within the heart of New Downtown Street, Toronto.

Our Founders:

Akash Tyagi: A visionary with an unwavering passion for culinary artistry, Akash Tyagi brings a wealth of creativity and expertise to Urban Spice Delight. With a background steeped in the rich culinary traditions of India, Akash masterfully crafts each dish to deliver a harmonious blend of traditional flavors and contemporary flair. His dedication to sourcing the finest ingredients and his keen attention to detail ensure that every plate is a true masterpiece. Derek Williamson: A culinary maverick with a global perspective, Derek Williamson complements the Urban Spice Delight experience with his diverse gastronomic influences. Having traversed the culinary landscapes of various continents, Derek infuses innovation and international zest into our menu. His commitment to pushing the boundaries of taste and presentation has earned Urban Spice Delight a reputation for being a hub of gastronomic exploration.

Our Journey:

Since our inception, Urban Spice Delight has remained dedicated to tantalizing taste buds and creating memories that linger long after the last bite. What started as a shared dream between Akash and Derek has blossomed into a culinary destination where patrons gather to indulge in a symphony of flavors, textures, and aromas.

Our Cuisine:

At Urban Spice Delight, we specialize in offering a diverse range of culinary delights inspired by the rich tapestry of global cuisines. From aromatic Indian curries that pay homage to Akash's roots, to inventive fusion dishes that reflect Derek's worldly experiences, our menu is a celebration of culinary fusion and creativity. Each dish is meticulously prepared, with a harmonious balance of spices and ingredients that dance on your palate, inviting you to embark on a journey of taste.

Our Ambiance:

Step into Urban Spice Delight and be transported into a world where contemporary elegance meets warm hospitality. Our restaurant is designed to provide a relaxed and inviting atmosphere, allowing you to savor every moment as you savor each dish. Whether you're enjoying an intimate dinner for two or celebrating a special occasion with loved ones, our welcoming ambiance sets the stage for a memorable dining experience.

Community and Beyond:

We believe in giving back to the community that has embraced us with open arms. Over the years, Urban Spice Delight has been involved in numerous philanthropic endeavors, supporting local initiatives and contributing to causes close to our hearts. We are committed to not only delighting your taste buds but also making a positive impact on the world around us. Join us at Urban Spice Delight, where passion meets culinary artistry, and every bite tells a story. Experience the fusion of flavors, cultures, and memories that make dining with us an Urban Spice Delight like no other.

Experience the Delight, Embrace the Urban Spice.
